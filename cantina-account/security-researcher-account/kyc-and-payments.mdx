## Know Your Customer

To receive payments you must first undergo the Know Your Customer process. This process is conducted using [withpersona.com](https://withpersona.com/). Cantina does not store any personal user information.

<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (36).png" alt="Security payment settings notification." />

After the identity verification process is complete, you will be able to set up your address and receive payments.
