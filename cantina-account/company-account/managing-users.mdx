# ðŸ”¹ Managing users

## Invite user

To add members to the registered company an account manager must

1. [Login](https://cantina.xyz/signup) with a client account.
2. Navigate to [cantina.xyz/client/profile](https://cantina.xyz/client/profile) > manage users.
3. Add the name and email of the person that must be invited to the company.

<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (39).png" alt="Security platform user invitation form." />

* Users invited to the company will have access to Cantina Repositories.&#x20;
* To revoke user access, click **Disable user** in the manage users page.

## Accept invitation

1. An email will be sent with a link to join a company.

<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (40).png" width="316" alt="Cantina platform invitation message." />

2. Accept the invitation and sign up using the same email where invitation was sent to. A registered user account with that email is waiting for you to claimed. [claim-an-account.md](../claim-an-account.md "mention").
