Follow the steps below to **create a new account**.

1. Navigate to [https://cantina.xyz/signup](https://cantina.xyz/signup) and introduce your email and new password of choice.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" alt="Web3 security platform Cantina sign up." width="294"><figcaption><p>Signup modal</p></figcaption></figure>

2. Verify your email and select "**I am a client**".

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" alt="Protocol security audit account options." width="367"><figcaption><p>Account selection</p></figcaption></figure>

3. Fill in the requested information:

* Your name
* Telegram username
* Discord username
* Company name
* Company email
* Company website
* Twitter username
* GitHub username

4. Customize your Company and Personal profiles.

{% hint style="success" %}
Now you are all set to use the Cantina application!
{% endhint %}
