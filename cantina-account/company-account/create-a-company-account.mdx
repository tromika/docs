# ðŸ”¹ Create a Company Account

<Note>
If a core team member has registered an account for you,  follow the steps to [claim-an-account.md](../claim-an-account.md "mention").
</Note>

Follow the steps below to **create a new account**.

1. Navigate to [https://cantina.xyz/signup](https://cantina.xyz/signup) and introduce your email and new password of choice.

<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" width="294" alt="Web3 security platform Cantina sign up." />

2. Verify your email and select "**I am a client**".

<img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" width="367" alt="Protocol security audit account options." />

3. Fill in the requested information:

* Your name
* Telegram username
* Discord username
* Company name
* Company email
* Company website
* Twitter username
* GitHub username

4. Customize your Company and Personal profiles.

<Note>
Now you are all set to use the Cantina application!
</Note>
