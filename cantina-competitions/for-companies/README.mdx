# üè¢ For Companies

[..](../ "mention") are a step in the development lifecycle that serve to increase the product's security posture before it reaches the market. Cantina Competitions are the best way to get a code base reviewed by a large number of people at once.

{% hint style="info" %}
Be sure to register a [company-account](../../cantina-account/company-account/ "mention") if you haven't done it yet.
{% endhint %}

At a high level these are the steps you will go through in the competition journey:

1. **Book competition.**
   * _Book a competition by filling in the_ [_request_](https://cantina.xyz/rfp/create) _form. A core team member will get in touch with you to inform you about next steps._
2. **Fill in scope and prize details.**
   * _Use the_ [competition-submission-template.md](competition-submission-template.md "mention")_._
3. **Accept and sign Statement Of Work.**
   * _Review and sign the competition proposal._
4. **Monitor** [comments-and-pings.md](../../cantina-code/cantina-code-for-security-researchers/comments-and-pings.md "mention") and [responding-to-findings.md](../../cantina-code/cantina-code-for-companies/responding-to-findings.md "mention").
   * _As findings come in, the Cantina team will handle the initial deduplication and filtering of issues. After this initial review, we will present you with a list of potential vulnerabilities. Your insights and comments on these findings will be highly valuable and can greatly enhance our decision-making._
5. [report-generation.md](../../cantina-code/cantina-code-for-companies/report-generation.md "mention") **and competition end.**
